About Me

I am a BS Economics and Mathematics (double major) student with a strong interest in data science and analytics. I work as an Artificial Intelligence intern at the National Aerospace Science and Technology Park (NASTP) and a Marketing Intern at My Restful Mind (MRM). I am a Google Certified Data Analyst after completing the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate. I am also enrolled in the Microsoft Power BI Data Analyst Professional Certificate course on Coursera, further enhancing my skills.

As an Executive Council Member of the IBA Economics Club and Assistant Director for the IBA Data Science Society, I am eager to apply my analytical expertise. I am committed to contributing to data-driven decision-making in dynamic professional settings and am open to collaborative opportunities while continuously learning in the evolving field of data science.
